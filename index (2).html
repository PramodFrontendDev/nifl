About NIFL

A coaching center built around one goal — getting you to speak, not just study.

NIFL English Speaking Classes is a trusted coaching center in Gwalior, Madhya Pradesh, known for a practical, conversation-first approach to learning English. Rather than focusing only on textbook grammar, our sessions are built around real speaking practice, personality development, and the confidence to use English in interviews, workplaces, and everyday life.

With 279+ Google reviews and a 4.8-star rating, NIFL has become a name local students and parents recognise — and trust.

  • Spoken English & fluency-focused curriculum
  • Small batches with individual speaking practice
  • Personality development & interview readiness
  • Certificate awarded on course completion

Leadership

Led by educators who believe confidence is taught, not born

NIFL English Speaking Classes was built on a simple observation: most students in Gwalior knew English grammar well, but froze when asked to actually speak. Our founding team set out to fix that gap directly — building a coaching style centered on practice, encouragement, and real conversation rather than rote learning.

That approach has earned NIFL recognition within the local education community, and — more importantly — the trust of hundreds of students who walked in hesitant and walked out fluent.
